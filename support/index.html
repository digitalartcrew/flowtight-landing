FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Is FlowTight free to use?

Yes. FlowTight is free to download and includes core features at no cost — recording, the beat maker, the step sequencer, and basic AI features.

FlowTight Pro is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited AI beat generation, full export, stem export, the complete vocal effects library, and commercial license documentation.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

FlowTight Pro is billed through Apple. To cancel, go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find FlowTight, and tap Cancel Subscription.

Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period. We do not offer refunds for partial billing periods, but if you believe you were charged in error please contact us.

Can I use my recordings commercially?

Yes. Every recording made in FlowTight receives an auto-generated license ID that documents your creation date and ownership. FlowTight Pro includes full commercial license documentation suitable for distribution on platforms like Spotify, Apple Music, and SoundCloud.

AI-generated beats produced within the app are licensed to you for commercial use. See our Terms of Service for full details.

How does AI beat generation work?

Tap the AI Beat feature, beatbox or hum a rhythm, choose a vibe, and optionally add a photo for visual inspiration. The AI generates a 30-second original beat in seconds.

Free accounts get a limited number of AI beat generations per month. FlowTight Pro includes unlimited generations.

How does the AI Lyric Generator work?

Choose a flow style (Standard, Triplet, Double Time, Syncopated, Melodic, Punchline, or Punches), enter your topic or theme, and the AI generates 4 bars written to match that rhythm pattern.

The generator works across all musical genres — not just hip-hop. Use it for pop hooks, R&B verses, country lyrics, and more.

My recording didn't save — what happened?

Recordings are saved to your account automatically when you finish. If a recording is missing, try the following:

1. Pull down to refresh your Library.
2. Check your internet connection — recordings sync when online.
3. Make sure you are signed into the same account you recorded with.

If the recording is still missing, email us with the approximate time and date of the recording and we'll investigate.

How do I export my beat or recording?

Export is available on FlowTight Pro. Open the recording or beat, tap the share icon, and choose Export Audio. You can export as a stereo mix or as individual stems (separate instrument tracks) for further production in other DAWs.

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion by emailing support@flowtight.app with the subject line "Delete My Account." Include the email address associated with your account.

Account deletion is permanent. All recordings, beats, and license records associated with your account will be removed within 30 days. See our Privacy Policy for details on data retention.

What iOS version does FlowTight require?

FlowTight requires iOS 16 or later. For the best experience with AI features, we recommend iOS 17+. Android support is planned for a future release.

I have a feature request or feedback.

We'd love to hear from you. Send feature requests and feedback to support@flowtight.app with the subject line "Feedback." Our team reads every message and uses your input to shape the product roadmap.
